When are most trips taken in terms of time of day, day of the week, or month of the year?¶

Have you ever wondered, when is the most trips taken at parts of the day, or parts of the week, or even parts of the year? Yeah, we too! Before we proceed to the findings, buckle up!

How long does the average trip take? Does the above depend on if a user is a subscriber or customer?¶

Next, we ask ourselves if the average trip taken is affected whether the user is a subscriber or customer. Surely, we find the answer!
